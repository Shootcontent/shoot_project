SHOOT. Photographic Studios

Booking & Payment

  • Full payment is required to confirm your booking.
  • Bookings are only secured once payment is received.
  • Session times include setup and breakdown, please plan accordingly.
  • Cancellations made less than 48 hours before the shoot are non-refundable. Rescheduling more than 48 hours before the shoot is subject to availability. Rescheduling within 48 hours of the shoot will incur a 50% fee. Rescheduling on the day of the shoot will be charged at 100% of the original fee.

Use of the Curve

  • The Curve is a specialty backdrop and must be treated with care.
  • No shoes, liquids, or any props that may mark or damage the Curve are allowed unless pre-approved.
  • Any damage caused to the Curve during your session will be assessed and billed accordingly.
  • A maximum of 10 people are allowed per booking. An additional fee of R50 per extra person will be charged for any attendees beyond this limit.

Lighting Equipment

  • Lighting gear is available for use or hire as agreed in your booking.
  • All equipment must be used responsibly. You will be held liable for any loss or damage during your session.
  • Please notify studio staff immediately if equipment appears faulty or becomes damaged.
  • We can hire certain equipment at an extra fee.

Studio Conduct

  • The studio must be left in the condition it was found.
  • All props & personal items must be removed, and surfaces wiped down if needed. Rubbish to be placed in the bins provided.
  • Any furniture or equipment moved during your session must be returned to its original position.

Overtime

  • Please respect your allocated time. You will be charged if you go over your booked time.
  • Overtime is billed in 15-minute increments and is subject to studio availability.

Liability

  • SHOOT. Studio is not liable for any injury, loss, or damage to personal property during your session.
  • Clients are responsible for any damage caused to the studio or equipment.

Usage & Rights

  • The studio is to be used for creative content production only. No unlawful or hazardous activity is permitted.
  • The space may not be sublet or shared without prior written consent.
  • Unless otherwise agreed, all intellectual property created by SHOOT. remains the property of the studio.

By booking with SHOOT. Studio, you agree to these terms and conditions.
